Why this matters

When insurers stop renewing policies, homeowners face a stark choice: find coverage on the expensive last-resort state FAIR plan, go uninsured, or sell. In Florida, where one-in-33 policies was not renewed in 2023, this is already reshaping property markets. Louisiana and North Carolina — both exposed to Gulf and Atlantic storms — rank close behind. California's volume (87,437 non-renewals) reflects the scale of wildfire risk repricing underway across the West.
